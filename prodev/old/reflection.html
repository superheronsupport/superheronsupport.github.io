Sample Reflection and Progress Timeline

Taking the time to reflect weekly, if not daily, is crucial to bootcamp participants success. Here is an example of a reflection also with a potential progress timeline for you to take into consideration.

Pre Bootcamp

Updated my Slack profile on deepdivecoding.slack.com with my full name and avatar, and mastered the basics of using the platform. Reviewed the following channels on our Slack team:
#deepdivecoding (Central channel for announcements affecting the entire Deep Dive community)
#tech-news-and-events (A place to share news related to tech industry and events happening in the community)
#career-job-postings (Message board to put career and job opportunities for all Deep Dive participants)
#random (channel for random quirky items, developer humor)

Week 1

Started working on first draft resume and online presence
Took a test and learned what my learning style is
Figured out some smart goals and my what, how, whys

Week 2

Got my tools and channels I am going to use for my online brand/presence
Had first meeting with coaches...
Designed/ordered business cards for myself

Week 3

Finished up my second revision of my resume and online presence
Formed team and did some team activities

Week 4

Finalized my resume and online presence. Now, I am creating a digital portfolio/resume for my website
Research companies in the local industry, started making personal contacts

Week 5

Getting my digital life organized. Got a splash page up for my website
Had second meeting with my coaches...

Week 6

Identified two local organizations (a for-profit and a non-profit) that I am interested in working with (as an employee or contractor) and write a simple cover letter expressing my interested in working with them.

Week 7

Start going to local meetups and connecting with the people in the evenings and weekends
Spent some time trying to connect with the local tech community and building relationships with potential mentors for the industry I am looking into

Week 8

Had a couple of HR and technical interviews...
Launched my final personal project/website

Week 9

Had final meeting with coaches...
Create/practice demos and elevator pitchers for graduation/demo day

Week 10

Started inviting professional contacts to Demo Day and Invited family and friends to Graduation

Post Bootcamp

Forked my capstone and started polishing it more
Participated in the Demo Days and Alumni Meetups.
Joined the #alumni-lounge channel on Slack. This is a channel for you to connect with other graduates and collaborate on initiatives.
Started working on a side project to continue learning
