Identify Two Orgs

The goal of this activity is to identify two organizations that you can connect with after your program to help them potentially with their technology needs.

Activity

Research two organizations. Preferably one for-profit company and one not-for-profit organization. Then, see if you have mutual connection there, utilized LinkedIn and your network.

Fill out this online survey once for each organization

Survey Monkey Form to fill out

Extra Credit
