Introduction

Hi, my name is Dan Heron. I am your Career Coach during the program and a Talent Manager you can utilize after the program. I created this professional development curriculum as a way to give bootcamp participants an introduction into what I believe it takes to succeed in the local tech industry. I have a variety of work experience from working with fast-paced tech startups to fortune 500 companies. You can get an idea of my background and connect with me via LinkedIn or Twitter @SuperHeronDan.

This is the home page for your professional development (aka Pro Dev) during the bootcamp. I maintain the website so if you see any errors or have any suggestions for improvement, don't hesitate to send me a message. It is a living document.
